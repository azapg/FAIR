Courses are the heart of the FAIR platform. They are where you organize your students, create assignments, and manage the grading process.

## Finding Your Courses

When you first log into the platform, **the Courses page is the first thing you see.** You don't need to look for it; it's your home base.

### Using the Sidebar
On the left side of the screen (the sidebar), you will see a "Courses" section.
- **Quick Access:** Your most recent or active courses will appear here so you can jump into them quickly.
- **See All:** If you have many courses, only the first few will show. Click the **"See all"** button at the bottom of that list to return to the main page and see every course you are part of.

## Starting a New Course

If you are an instructor and want to set up a new class:

1. Go to the **Courses** page.
2. Click the **Create** button (look for the plus icon).
3. Type in the **Name** of your course (e.g., "Introduction to AI - Fall 2026").
4. Add a short **Description** if you'd like.
5. Click **Create**.

## Inviting Students

Every course has a unique **Enrollment Code**. This is a simple 4-letter code (like `B3KF`) that identifies your class.

- **To invite students:** Just give them this code. They will enter it on their own Courses page to join your class.
- **Changing the code:** If you ever need to stop new people from joining or want a new code, you can change these settings inside the course dashboard.

## What's Inside a Course?

Once you've created a course, you can click on it to start adding content:
- **Assignments:** Create the tasks your students need to complete.
- **Submissions:** See the work your students have turned in.
- **Grading:** Set up how you want the AI or yourself to grade the work.
